GREET - Beta Release Septeber 21st 2012 Workshop

This workshop held at Argonne National Laboratory was introducing the new GREET platform for the first time to the general public. A full day of presentation with the objective of introducing all the features of this software to our users and get their feedback.

As a reminder, issues or suggestions can be reported on our bug tracking system, and documentation for the software can be downloaded from here Some of Friday's suggestions are already corrected and ready to be published for our next update on Thursday27.
