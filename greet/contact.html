GREET development team

The Systems Assessment Section of the Energy Systems Division at Argonne will continue to work on improving the data and enhance the software.

Given for free to the community, Argonne's research team is actively maintaining the software. For any remarks or suggestions please send an email to greet@anl.gov.

You can also chat about GREET online on our irc webchat.
